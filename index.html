How It Works

01
🎙️

Audio Input

Record or upload a speech audio file. The system accepts WAV, MP3, or OGG formats.

→
02
📊

MFCC Extraction

40 Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients are extracted from 3 seconds of audio using librosa.

→
03
🧠

Neural Network

A 3-layer Dense network (256→128→8) with Dropout predicts the emotion class.

→
04
✨

Emotion Result

One of 8 emotions is returned: neutral, calm, happy, sad, angry, fearful, disgust, or surprised.
